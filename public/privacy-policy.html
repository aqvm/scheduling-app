Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DnD Group Scheduler ("the app") handles your information when you use the scheduling app.

Information Collected

  • Google account identity details used for sign-in (such as your email address).
  • Profile information you provide in the app (such as display name).
  • Campaign membership and invite-code usage data.
  • Availability selections you submit for calendar dates.

How Information Is Used

  • To authenticate you and allow secure access to the app.
  • To display scheduling and availability information to campaign members.
  • To support admin and host functions (campaign management and summaries).
  • To maintain and secure app operations.

Data Sharing

Your data is shared only with users who are members of the same campaign and with service providers required to run the app (for example, Firebase/Google Cloud). Data is not sold.

Data Retention

Data is retained while campaigns remain active. Campaign admins may remove campaign members and delete campaigns, which removes associated campaign data from the app database.

Security

The app uses Firebase Authentication and Firestore security rules to help protect data. No system can guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can ask a campaign admin to remove you from a campaign.
  • You can request deletion of your data by contacting the app operator.
